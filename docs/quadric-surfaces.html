Quadric Surfaces

General form of quadric surfaces:

Summary

Equation Characteristic Surface Orientation
No minus signs Ellipsoid
No linear terms Elliptic cone Opens along axis of negative term
Only two terms Cylinder Runs along axis of missing term
One minus sign One-sheet hyperboloid Opens along axis of negative term
Two-minus signs Two-sheet hyperboloid Opens along axis of positive term
One linear term and two quadratic terms with same sign Elliptic Parabloid Opens along axis of linear term
One inear terms, two quadratic terms with opposite signs Hyperbolic Parabloid Opens along axis of linear term (both ways, 90 degrees offset from each other)
