Soil Formation

Soil is a complex mixture of:

  • Minerals
  • Organic particles
  • Air
  • Water
  • Microorganisms
  • Decaying organisms

Derived from deposition of weathered and eroded rock

Parent material - base rock for soil

Factors that influence soil formation:

  • Climate
  • Organisms
  • Topography
  • Parent material
  • Time
